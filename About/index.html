ABOUT ME

Howdy, mate! a 14 Year Old Web Developer & Graphics Designer hailing from the Maldives, where flip-flops are the epitome of formal wear. Picture me, coding away with a coconut in one hand and a keyboard in the other.

It all began when my awesome dad got me a Raspberry Pi 3, a small computer. Excited to explore, I opened a drawing app because I loved drawing. Little did I know, this tiny computer would change my life. A few days later, while playing around, I stumbled upon an app with a cute cat icon I clicked on it, thinking it was a game.

To my surprise, it wasn't a game but a whole new world of coding. Instantly, I fell in love with Scratch. My first project I made was was a ping pong game that I made with my cousin, And that click set the stage for my entire journey.

Sun, Jan 31, 2021, was the day I created my first Scratch account, named yunus4040. Little did I know that within a year, I would become the most followed user in the Maldives! Imagine me, a little kid, creating games and gaining so many fans â€“ it's like being the coolest kid in the digital neighborhood.

A few years later, I found myself stuck in my cousin's house due to the rain. He was busy coding a website, I decided to sit next to him and observe. Turns out, my cousin was working on a simple HTML web page. Intrigued by this, I got inspired to create my own website called "SeekGames," because my dad and uncle's one's made this site named "SeekJobs," a platform to searche for jobs.

Once the rain stopped, I went home and expressed my interest to my dad about creating a website. He downloaded an application Brackets on his MacBook, and I began my journey into website development. Although I didn't manage to complete the SeekGames website, the experience taught me valuable coding skills.

Fast forward to today, I am still that 14-year-old coding enthusiast from the Maldives, but now, my skills have evolved beyond Scratch games on a Raspberry Pi. I've delved into the world of web development and graphic design, creating my own digital footprint in the vast expanse of the internet.
